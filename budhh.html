About Buddh International Circuit

Buddh International Circuit is India's premier Formula 1 racing track, located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh. It has been the venue for several major motorsport events, including the Formula 1 Indian Grand Prix.

The Track

The Buddh International Circuit spans 5.125 kilometers with 16 turns, offering one of the fastest and most thrilling circuits in the world. It challenges drivers with high-speed straights, tight corners, and elevation changes.
