About Expo Mart

India Expo Mart (commonly referred to as India Exposition Mart or India Expo Centre & Mart) is a premier exhibition and convention venue located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, near the national capital, New Delhi. It is one of India's largest integrated venues for trade fairs, conferences, exhibitions, and business events, catering to both national and international events.
